About me

I am Sandra Macias. I studied philosoophy, work as an English teacher, play the bass and the uke in my free time and am currently learning how to make websites.

About this website

This website is designed to keep all the information my band needs to plan its rehearsals, to keep track of our setlist and to listen to our recordings. It is my final project at the Holberton School Bootcamp for women which took place in Medellin in December 2019.
